CAR CARE TIPS

As your motor car takes a backseat for the moment to prepare for its next journey, here are some tips from us to keep your Rolls-Royce in tip-top condition.

Protect Your Investment

Keep your Rolls-Royce paintwork in pristine condition by protecting it with your choice of indoor and outdoor car cover options.

Keep Clean On The Inside

Maintain the cleanliness of all interior surfaces with the appropriate alcohol-free cleaning products. Contact us for detailed grooming tips!

Maintain Battery Condition

Maintain optimum charge in your battery by connecting it to a battery conditioner; or run the engine for 15 - 20 minutes once or twice a week, if possible

Flatten The Curve, Not Your Tyres

Prevent flat tyre spots from forming during extended periods of storage by using tyre cradles, or move the car' s position once or twice a week.
